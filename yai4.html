General Insurance Claim Process

You are just a single click away from a comprehensive General Insurance claim settlement

In our endeavour to provide you the best coverage along with a host of other value added services, our online General Insurance claims system has been designed keeping your ease in mind. With a convenient insurance claim process, you can now register your claim, upload the necessary documents and know the status instantly.

What You Will Need to Provide

When we say hassle-free, trust us to mean every syllable of it. We realize that your time is precious, particularly during an emergency. What you would have to provide is therefore minimal.

Vehicle Insurance Claims

  • • Vehicle Registration/Plate Number
  • • Vehicle Color and Model
  • • Insurance Policy Number
  • • Engine and Chassis Number
  • • Contact Number
  • • Date and Time of Incident
  • • Description and Location of Incident
  • • Vehicle Inspection Address
  • • Kilometer Reading
  • • Staff ID (if applicable)

Home Insurance Claims

  • • Property Address (Complete)
  • • Insurance Policy Number
  • • Contact Number
  • • Date and Time of Incident
  • • Description of Damage
  • • Estimated Damage Value
  • • Staff ID (if applicable)
  • • Property Type and Size
  • • Cause of Damage
  • • Emergency Contact Details
